50km.

May 50km is a fundraising initiative from Multiple Sclerosis Research Australia and encourages participants to run or walk 50km during the month of May. We'll be doing ours a little differently by running the full 50km in the one day, around our work block (approx 200m per lap). By passing by our office, and the local businesses and cafes, every 60sec or so we're aiming to bring visibility to this invisible illness.
